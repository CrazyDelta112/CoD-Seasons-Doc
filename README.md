## Season 1

<p align="center">
<img src="https://codmwstore.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/Season1-scaled.jpg" width="2400">
</p>

---

### Overview

Season One is the first batch of post-launch content for Call of Duty: Modern Warfare that was released in December 3rd, 2019 and ended on February 10th, 2020.

The content is focused on the story taking place after the successful Armistice's Operations in Verdansk, with the escalating tension between Allegiance and Coalition forces and the discovery of a potential deadly weapon linked with Al-Asad whereabouts.

The first patch at launch brought some maps across the Multiplayer and Spec Ops game modes, the new Battle Pass system alongside the store and cosmetic items, while a "Refresh" update was released in December 18, 2019 with the remaining content scheduled for this season.

## Season 2

<p align="center">
<img src="https://www.gamespot.com/a/uploads/original/1578/15787979/3635315-s2_roadmap.jpg" width="2400">
</p>

---

### Overview

Season Two is the second batch of post-launch content for Call of Duty: Modern Warfare that released in February 11th, 2020 and ended on April 7th, 2020.

The second major batch of content focuses around Simon Riley and content themed around his past, including Mace, a former ally of Simon, as well as a series of events involving Armistice operators taking place in Verdansk after several Operations were conducted against Al-Qatala forces as a consequence for the Invasion of Verdansk. 

---

### Extra

Season 2 was the most special season because it was when the Warzone mode was officially released to the public, launching with the famous **Verdansk** map.
